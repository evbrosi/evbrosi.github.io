Hello,

my name is Eagle Valiant Brosi. I makes comics, zines, and graphic novels.

I was raised in an unconventional, back-to-the-land hippie lifestyle in the Appalachian foothills. Despite the non-traditional beginnings, I conformed to the standard life of an American capitalist. And now work from home, making a computer sigh and groan.

The garden provides a contrast to the quiet work life. Among rows of tomatoes, beans, zucchini, and summer squash, I enjoy the good company of slugs, mosquitoes, and bumblebees.
