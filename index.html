EAGLE VALIANT BROSI

makes comics, zines, and graphic novels.

His debut graphic novel, Black Cohosh, is a 2026 LA Times Book Award Finalist.

Eagle Valiant Brosi was raised in an unconventional, back-to-the-land hippie lifestyle in the Appalachian foothills. Despite his non-traditional beginnings, Eagle conformed to the standard life of an American capitalist. He works from home, making a computer sigh and groan.

His garden provides a contrast to the quiet work life. Among rows of tomatoes, beans, zucchini, and summer squash, he enjoys the good company of slugs, mosquitoes, and bumblebees.
